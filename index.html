SUGGEST A NAME

Things we would love our wine name to consider:

A.S. Initials

Many of our family members have the initials A.S., so having a wine that contain those letters or initials would mean a lot.

SONCINI Surname

A wine that includes our surname (or its variations, such as "SON" etc) would be very welcome.

Bilingual Aspect

Ideally, we would love a name easy to pronounce in both English and Portuguese (our native language).

Positivity

Although the wine name won't necessarily need to mean anything, having a positive connotation will be a plus.
